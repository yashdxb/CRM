## Purpose

Define minimum hygiene and progression expectations for opportunity stages.

## Baseline rules

1. Opportunities must have current stage and expected close date.
2. Each active opportunity must include a next step and due date.
3. Required stage fields must be completed before progression.

## Manager checks

1. Flag opportunities with stale activity.
2. Flag opportunities without next step.
