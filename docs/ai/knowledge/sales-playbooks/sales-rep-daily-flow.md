## Purpose

Define expected daily flow for sales reps in Command Center.

## Daily sequence

1. Review overdue and due-today tasks.
2. Work no-next-step and at-risk opportunities.
3. Log activity outcomes and schedule next actions.
4. Update stage, close date, and forecast category where needed.

## Minimum standards

1. Do not leave active opportunities without next step.
2. Activity outcomes should be concise and factual.
