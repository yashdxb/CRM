## Purpose

Define daily manager execution routine for forecast discipline and team coaching.

## Daily manager workflow

1. Review team pipeline and late-stage concentration.
2. Inspect opportunities with no next step or stale activity.
3. Review approvals queue and pending escalation items.
4. Validate commit and forecast category quality.
5. Create coaching tasks with clear owner and due date.
6. Track rep acknowledgment for review outcomes.

## Review priorities

1. Stuck-stage opportunities.
2. Large deals close to expected close date.
3. Deals with missing execution evidence.
4. Opportunities waiting on approval decisions.

## Manager outcomes

1. Coaching actions assigned and tracked.
2. Risk deals moved into recovery plans.
3. Forecast view reflects validated execution status.
